How to play

×

Introduction

The goal of the game is to defeat all the enemies in the barbarian camps outside of the castle.

Movment

The player moves using W,S,D,A keys and attack with the Space key.

Camera

Player camera can be rotated by holding the left mouse key and moving the mouse. Pressing R will reset the camera to its original position.

Health

The player has a health bar, getting hit by enemies will result in the player loosing health points, these can be restored by finding hearts scattered around the map.

Music

Pressing the M key will play background music.
